About The Quilt Room

The Quilt Room are patchwork and quilting specialists trading for over 37 years. We are mum and daughter team helped with a wonderful group of staff – some of whom have been with us from the beginning! We are based in a lovely Surrey village called Beare Green, not far from the south of London (J9 M25).

We sell an eclectic mix of high quality modern and traditional quilting & patchwork fabric from well loved brands such as FreeSpirit Fabrics, Moda, Dashwood Studios, Makower, Andover, Windham, Blend and many more, as well as a variety of jelly rolls and quilting supplies. We are also the authors behind the Jelly Roll Quilt book series and we sell many exclusive kits to make the patchwork quilts from our quilting books.

You & The Quilt Room

We can help you on your journey whether it be teaching you new techniques in one of our workshops to quilting your quilt top on one of our two professional computerised Gammill longarm quilting machines.

Our friendly team have a wealth of knowledge and are always on hand to help and make your time with us enjoyable. We welcome groups to our quilting shop – just let us know if your group wishes to visit and we’ll pop the kettle on and get the biscuits out!

We warmly welcome you to our website and we hope you enjoy browsing, reading our blog and visiting our Instagram and Facebook page!

Pam & Nicky x
